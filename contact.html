What to Include

To help us understand your project and provide an accurate estimate, please include the following when reaching out:

  • Project Type: Content creation, private commission, etc.
  • Scope: Approximate size and complexity of the build, special requirements, game version, etc.
  • Timeline: Any deadlines or preferred delivery dates
  • References: Images, videos, or descriptions that capture your vision
  • Budget Range: Helps us tailor our approach to your needs

Don't worry if you're still figuring things out… we're happy to help shape ideas into concrete plans during our initial conversation!

Response Time

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent projects, please mention your timeline in your initial message and we'll do our best to prioritize your request.
