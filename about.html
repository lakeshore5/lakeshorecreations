About Us

What We Do

We are a full-service Minecraft build studio, helping content creators, brands, and studios ideate, build, and deliver iconic worlds. With 10 build leads bringing 75+ years of combined experience, we turn ambitious visions into reality.

What makes us different starts outside the game. Our team includes a full-time architectural designer with 9 real-world architecture publications and a Master of Architecture degree, alongside a full-time software developer who understands the technical constraints and possibilities of game design. This isn't just about placing blocks—it's about understanding space, form, and how environments shape experience.

Our leads have years of experience building with top content creators, including MrBeast. Every project begins with understanding your video concept, audience, and the feeling the space should evoke—turning good ideas into iconic backdrops that elevate your content.

We've shipped content to the official Minecraft Marketplace, meeting Microsoft's quality standards and technical requirements. From adventure maps to survival spawns, we understand what it takes to create commercially successful Minecraft content.

From intimate server spawns to sprawling adventure maps, we've delivered countless private commissions. Several of our build leads have been shaping Minecraft's building landscape since its earliest days, understanding what makes communities thrive.

We've created worlds for NFT Worlds and other blockchain-based Minecraft experiences. We understand the unique requirements of Web3 projects and how to build environments that serve both gameplay and community ownership.

When the stakes are high and deadlines are real, teams call us because they know we deliver. We work with brands and production studios to create bespoke Minecraft experiences that align with campaign goals and brand guidelines.

Whether you're planning your next video, launching a server, or dreaming up something that doesn't exist yet—we'd love to hear about it.
